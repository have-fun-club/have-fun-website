The Vibe

Autonomous FPV drone racing is a very stupid and very cool idea. We're leaning in. The goal is to win the AI Grand Prix. The process is to build the best possible RL policy and hardware stack, learn everything, and have fun doing it.

The Stack

Training on Isaac Lab with custom drone physics — real wind disturbance, gate navigation, adversarial conditions. The simulation has to be brutal so the real thing is easy. Or at least less crashy.

The Competition

AI Grand Prix — November 2026. $500,000 prize pool. We're going for it. No hedging, no backup plan.
